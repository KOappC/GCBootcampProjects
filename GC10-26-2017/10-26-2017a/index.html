You want to check if your group of ten friends qualify to receive a discount on tickets to the Lions game. Declare a variable called numberOfPeople and initialize it to the value of 10. You can receive a discount if your group is larger than 7 people.

Write an if statement that checks if your group is larger than 7 people. It should the statement, "You have enough people to get a discount!" if you qualify. If not, it should say, "Sorry, you need more people in your group!"
